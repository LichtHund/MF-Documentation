# Triumph GUI

### What is Triumph-GUI?

This lib was made to simplify the creation of GUIs.

### **Features** <a id="why-mf"></a>

* Simple to use.
* Per item action.
* Slot specific actions.
* Close and Open actions.
* Pagination GUI.
* Scrolling GUI.
* Item Builder.
